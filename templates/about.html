AES (Advanced Encryption Standard):
Symmetric-key algorithm for secure data transmission. Operates on fixed-size blocks of data using a series of substitution, permutation, and mixing operations.
RSA (Rivest-Shamir-Adleman)
Asymmetric-key algorithm for encryption and digital signatures. Uses the difficulty of factoring large numbers into prime factors as the basis for its security.
Blowfish
Symmetric-key algorithm with fast encryption and flexibility. Works on variable-length blocks, employing a Feistel network structure combined with key-dependent S-boxes for encryption and decryption.
